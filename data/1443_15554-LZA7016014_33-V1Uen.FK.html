class SctpEndpoint

ManagedElement
Transport
SctpEndpoint
SctpAssociation [0..]

Represents logical sender and receiver of SCTP packets.

Maximum number of SctpEndpoint MO in node is limited to 128.
Each SctpEndpoint is served by SCTP server assigned to one Router MO.

Maximum number of SCTP servers running in parallel is limited to:
- For Baseband in RAN Compute Group 1, 2, or 3: 8,
- For Baseband in RAN Compute Group 4 or higher: 12.
