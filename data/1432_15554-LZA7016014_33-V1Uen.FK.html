class LagPort

ManagedElement
Transport
LagPort

Represents group of Ethernet link ports bundled together to act as single link, to provide link redundancy and increased bandwidth.

Dependencies: EthernetPort MO that is encapsulated by LagPort MO, cannot be used for MO types VlanPort, Dot1xSupplicant, InterfaceIPv4 or InterfaceIPv6.

encapsulation
List of references to underlying encapsulations.

Underlying encapsulations can be EthernetPort MOs.

LACP is in active mode when enabled.
